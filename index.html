About the Doremi Quartet

The DoReMi Quartet is a talented group of young musicians based in London. Formed in 2014, they have played in concert halls around the world and received multiple awards from the North London Festival of Music.

Aki Bartlett

Aki plays violin.

Anabel Hannay

Anabel plays violin and viola.

Akari Honda

Akari plays cello.

Leo Bartlett

Leo plays viola and violin.

News and Updates

  • 19 May 2017 — DoReMi Quartet Performed at the Royal Albert Hall's Verdi Restaurant

    Thank you to all those who came to see us perform our lunchtime concert at the Royal Albert Hall! And a big thank you to Music For Youth for such a wonderful opportuntiy. Videos and photos coming soon…

  • 9 May 2017 — DoReMi Wins Stringers of London Award for Most Outstanding Ensemble

    Following our performance at the North London Festival last weekend, we were thrilled to hear today that we have won the Stringers of London Award for the most outstanding string ensemble in the Festival. All our thanks to the sponsors and judges for their recognition and encouragement.

  • 8 May 2017 — DoReMi Wins Gold Medal at the North London Festival of Music

    DoReMi played in the String Trios/Quartets (12 years and under) category at the North London Festival last Saturday. The competition was very stiff so we were delighted to receive a gold medal. Hooray!
